    How AI is Changing Web Development

    By Muhammad Hasnain | Technology

    Artificial Intelligence is transforming the way websites and applications are built. From smart code completion to automated testing, AI tools are helping developers work faster and more efficiently.

    Modern frameworks and AI-powered platforms allow developers to focus more on creativity and problem-solving instead of repetitive tasks.

    In the future, AI will play an even bigger role in web development, enabling personalized user experiences and smarter applications.
